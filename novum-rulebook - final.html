Operating Manual
The Founding Rulebook

The complete framework for how NOVUM operates, earns, and grows together.

Version 2.0 June 2026 4 Co-founders Contribution Model

Why this version exists: After honest discussion, the team agreed that since no founder is currently fully committed to NOVUM as their primary focus, a passive share is not justified. This version moves to a pure contribution model — you earn only what you do. The company grows as a shared portfolio and brand that benefits everyone in the long run.

01

Core Principles

  • You earn what you contribute — nothing more, nothing less
  • Revenue generation is directly rewarded
  • The NOVUM brand and portfolio is the shared long-term asset for all founders
  • Transparency is mandatory in every deal, every payment, every decision
  • All 4 founders are equal co-owners — but ownership does not generate income unless you contribute
02

Company Structure

All 4 founders are equal co-owners of NOVUM regardless of current personal situation. Ownership is permanent unless a founder formally exits per Section 19.

03

Revenue Distribution Model

Every project payment is split in the following fixed order:

1
Company Fund — 5%

Taken first from every payment. Used for hosting, domains, software, AI tools, marketing, and infrastructure.

2
External Costs (if any)

Outsourcing fees or third-party costs deducted before personal earnings are calculated.

3
Remaining Amount Split

Distributed according to roles as outlined below.

Who Share For What
Work Executor70%Doing the actual project work
Client Connector25%Bringing the client
Company Fund5%Taken first, from total payment
Special Rule: If the same person both executes the work AND brought the client, they receive 95%, and 5% goes to Company Fund. Any rounding difference also goes to Company Fund.
04

Course Work — Payment Structure

Teaching, syllabus creation, and content delivery is higher-effort work and is compensated at a slightly higher rate. For every course batch or sale, after the 10% Company Fund is taken first:

WhoShareFor What
Course Creator / Teacher70%Teaching, syllabus, content, delivery
Student Connector25%Enrolling / bringing the student
Company Fund (additional)5%Brand, portfolio
ℹ️

Support roles (admin, enrollment, routing, scheduling) done by other founders are considered baseline NOVUM contribution — not separately billable. If support workload becomes unusually heavy, a one-time bonus from Company Fund may be approved by majority vote.

If course is outsourced: External instructor is paid an agreed fixed fee first (External Cost). Remaining follows the standard course split above. The founder who manages the outsourced instructor receives the 70% executor share.

05

Website Project — Payment Structure

  • Standard distribution from Section 3 applies to all website projects
  • Only one founder executes a website project at a time — that person is the Lead Developer
  • Assignment is decided based on availability, skill, and deadline — agreed and logged before work begins
  • No founder starts a website project without it being formally assigned
06

Client Acquisition — Everyone's Responsibility

There is no dedicated salesperson. Every founder is expected to bring clients through: cold calling, LinkedIn and Instagram outreach, referrals, personal networking, WhatsApp follow-ups, and any other approach.

The 25% sales commission is a strong incentive — whoever brings the client, earns it. This applies equally to website projects and course enrollments.

If a client comes through the NOVUM website or social media without a specific founder's direct effort, the 25% sales commission goes to Company Fund.

07

Ghost Client Protection

🛡️

Non-negotiable: No founder should lose days or weeks of work because a client disappears.

Before any work begins, all three must be confirmed:

  • Written brief — client requirements confirmed in writing (WhatsApp is acceptable)
  • Advance payment — minimum 40% of agreed total received before work starts
  • Project logged — entry in shared tracker with client name, contact, amount, advance received, deadline
40%
Project Start — Advance
60%
Final Delivery — Balance
⚠️

Final files, domain access, and hosting credentials are never handed over until 100% payment is received.

If client ghosts mid-project: Work stops immediately. Lead Developer is compensated from advance: 90% to Lead Developer, 10% to Company Fund. One final written message is sent, then project is closed.

If client ghosts before work starts: The advance rule means no work was done — no loss incurred. Log the lead as Dead in the tracker and move on.

10

Role Flexibility

There are no permanently fixed execution roles. Any founder can take a website project, bring a client, handle course administration, manage social media, or handle client communication. Earnings always follow contribution.

11

Social Media & Brand Contribution

  • Posting and managing NOVUM social media is a baseline founder duty — not separately paid
  • Only approved content may be posted on official NOVUM accounts
  • Any founder posting on behalf of NOVUM must inform the group beforehand
12

Client Ownership Rule

All clients acquired using the NOVUM name, website, brand, or social media belong to NOVUM — not to any individual.

  • No private handling of company clients
  • No redirecting clients to personal freelance work
  • No using NOVUM client contacts for personal projects
⚠️

If a founder uses NOVUM's brand for a personal deal without disclosure: 5% of total project value must be paid to Company Fund. If hidden and discovered later — formal warning issued and Company Fund compensation required.

13

Outsourcing Rules

A
Outsourced Execution

External person does the work. They are paid first as External Cost. Remaining follows standard split.

B
External Person Brings Client

Project stays under NOVUM. External person is paid from the 25% sales commission. Remaining distribution is standard.

C
External Partner Joint Deal Using NOVUM Brand

Treated as a full company project. Standard rules apply.

14

Company Fund Management

💰

Company Fund withdrawals above ₹500 require approval from at least 2 founders.

15

Communication Rules

  • One official NOVUM Founders Group (WhatsApp or Telegram) for all company communication
  • No company decision made outside this group is binding
  • Every founder must respond within 24 hours on normal days
  • If unavailable for more than 2 days, inform the group in advance
  • Internal finances, conflicts, and problems are never discussed with outsiders
16

Commitment Rule

Since there is no passive company share, there is no penalty for being inactive — you simply earn nothing during that period. However:

  • A founder consistently inactive for 60+ days with zero contribution must communicate their situation to the group
  • If unreachable for 60+ days with no prior notice, the team may vote to reassign pending tasks and reduce access to company accounts until they return
  • Ownership is not affected by inactivity unless the founder formally exits
17

Conflict Resolution

  • All conflicts are discussed openly in the founders group
  • Both sides must be heard before any decision is made
  • Majority vote (3 of 4) applies
  • If unresolved after majority discussion, the decision is paused for 7 days and revisited
  • No founder may disrespect another founder publicly or in front of clients
18

Future Services

As NOVUM expands into AI integration, automation, and custom software:

  • New pricing standards will be added to this document
  • Role assignments for new services are decided by majority before the service is publicly offered
  • No new service is advertised until at least one founder can confidently deliver it
